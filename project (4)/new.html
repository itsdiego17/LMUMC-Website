"Everyone is welcome here"

All children are welcome to attend the various services with their parents. Nursery service is available for children birth to age 3 every Sunday morning from 9:00 to 11:30. During the 10:15 and 11:15 worship services, children are often invited to the chancel to "Moments with Children", a time during which one of the pastors or Children's Director sits down with the children and briefly talks with them about a topic based on the day's sermon.

Open Hearts..., Open Minds..., Open Doors...

Open Hearts... We strive to open our hearts to the world and accept each person as a child of God.

Open Minds... We challenge ourselves to consider new perspectives while honoring our biblical tradition. Our church is based on thoughtful and heartfelt faith.

Open Doors... Everyone is welcomed to worship with us, to participate in the sacrament of communion, to join us in embracing and embodying the diversity of God's family.

Our Mission is at the heart of all we do: To make disciples of Jesus Christ!

As United Methodists, we understand that our openness to new people, ideas, and ways of reaching out to help others defines who we are. Welcoming differences, considering opposing reviews, and embracing change amount to some of the toughest things we do, and they are also some of the most important.
