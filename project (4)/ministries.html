Room in the Inn

We prepare a meal once per month, pick up homeless men from Area Relief Ministries, fellowship with them, and they spend the night in our church.

Life House Recovery

Lifehouse is part of Aspell Recovery Center. Women from Lifehouse participate in Zumba classes in our gym, paid for by members of the LMUMC. The Church administers a clothes closet and a card ministry for the women.

Meals on Wheels

Meals on Wheels are delivered by some of our members.

Society of Stephen

The Church helps needy persons through a fund (donations made to the Society of Stephen) administered by the Pastor and church office.
